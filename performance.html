The CedCommerce Team is glad to inform you that you can now create the newly launched Performance Max Campaign through our Suite for Google Shopping Feed App easily and seamlessly in order to showcase your products to get potential traffic and sales to your online store.

Performance Max is a new campaign type in Google Ads that maximizes your performance across all Google inventory using machine learning, and is a fully automated campaigns. It helps you drive performance based on your specified conversion goals, delivering more conversions and value by optimizing performance in real-time and across channels using Smart Bidding


To know more about Performance Max Campaigns Click Here

How to Create a Performance Max Campaign in just 4 easy steps through the App-

  • Open the Suite for Google Shopping Feed Application in your store from Apps Section, or “click here”
  • Click on Google Ads on the left panel menu
  • Click on Performance Max Campaigns tab
  • Enter the 4 easy inputs:
    • Create a Campaign name
    • Enter the Daily budget
    • Set Target location
    • click on Create Performance Max Campaign button.
